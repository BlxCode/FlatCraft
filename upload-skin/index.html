How to make a skin?

  1. Download this template
  2. Open your favorite pixel art editor, and edit the poses. Remember that each pose can be as big as 20x22 pixels. The game adjusts the player's hitbox from the size of your skin
  3. Export your skin, and upload it! (You can update a skin if you have the update key)
